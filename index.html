About Me

I'm currently studying Systems Analysis and Development (Anhanguera), and I'm also a Bachelor in Biological Sciences (UNESP/2016) and Master in Psychobiology (UFRN/2019).

  • Good communication skills in Portuguese and English.
  • Teamwork and analytical skills.
